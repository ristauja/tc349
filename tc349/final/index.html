Cloud 9 is one of the premier teams in the competitive scene of the massively popular online game League of Legends. Cloud 9 competes in the North American League Championship series and has been the number one team in North America for two years straight. They are known for their innovation, team play, and dominance in North America. By visiting the links above you will get a more in-depth look at both the players individually and the team as a whole.
